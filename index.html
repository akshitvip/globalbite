Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Our policy ensures:

  • No Location Tracking: We do NOT request GPS permissions. We rely on manual address input to protect user privacy.
  • Data Collection: We only collect Name, Phone Number, and Address essential to fulfill and deliver your food orders.
  • Secure Payments: Your information is never shared with third parties, except for payment processing via PayU's secure servers.
  • Data Protection: All transactions follow RBI guidelines and are processed through 256-bit SSL encrypted channels.

Refund & Technical Errors

Food items are perishable. Our policy regarding PayU transactions is as follows:

  • Cancellation: You can cancel your order until it is "Accepted" by our kitchen. Once accepted, preparation starts and cancellation is no longer possible.
  • Failed Transactions: If an amount is deducted but the order is not confirmed due to a technical error, PayU will automatically initiate a refund.
  • Technical Glitches: In case of double payments or system errors, refunds will be processed back to the original payment source after verification.
  • Timeline: Approved refunds are processed immediately but may take 5-7 business days to reflect in your bank account as per standard banking norms.
