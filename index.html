Refund & Cancellation

Food items are perishable, and therefore our policy is strict but fair:

Cancellation Logic

  • Pre-Acceptance: You can cancel your order only if it has NOT been accepted by our Admin/Kitchen.
  • Post-Acceptance: Once the Admin accepts the order, it enters the preparation stage and cannot be cancelled through the app.
  • Manual Intervention: If an order was placed by mistake and accepted, you must immediately contact our Support Team via email or visit our outlet. If preparation has started, cancellation will be denied.

Refund Rules

  • Refunds are only issued for non-delivery of items or delivery of incorrect products.
  • If an order is marked "Delivered" in our system, it is considered a completed transaction.
  • Approved refunds will be credited back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
